考虑 $(r,\theta)$ 体积微元的贡献为: $dV = dr \cdot r d\theta \cdot 2\pi \cdot r\sin(\theta) $

设 $r = r(\theta)$，那么体积就是:
$$
\int_{r = 0}^{r = r(\theta)} \int_{\theta = 0}^{\theta = \theta_0} dV
$$
先把 $r$ 积出来，所以 $$V = \int_{\theta = 0}^{\theta = \theta_0} {\frac{2\pi}{3} r^3 \sin(\theta) d\theta} $$
